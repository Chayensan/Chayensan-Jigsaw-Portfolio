# JIGSAW — WORLD SYSTEM.md

## OVERVIEW

Jigsaw is not a portfolio.

It is an atmosphere.

A living archive of how one person moves through rooms, ideas, systems, people, and energy.

This is not a website about jobs, titles, or perfectly packaged expertise.

It is about emotional architecture.

The invisible conditions that make people:
stay longer,
trust deeper,
gather naturally,
and remember how something felt long after it ended.

The person behind this portfolio does not position herself as the loudest person in the room.

She positions herself as someone who understands:
why the room matters.

Every design choice should reinforce this subconsciously.

The viewer should leave feeling:

> “This person notices things most people miss.”

And more importantly:

> “I want her around the things I’m building.”

---

# CORE POSITIONING

## NOT A DESIGNER PORTFOLIO

Not:

* “Look at my projects.”
* “Look at my metrics.”
* “Look at my experience.”

Instead:

> “This is the atmosphere I know how to create around people.”

The portfolio exists in the space between:

* design
* systems
* emotion
* strategy
* culture

* energy

It should feel discovered, not marketed.

# THE CENTRAL METAPHOR

## THE JIGSAW PUZZLE

The entire portfolio is built around one emotional structure:

Growth is not linear.

Identity is not linear.

Careers are not linear.

People become themselves through fragments.

Interior design.
Community building.
Events.
Spatial thinking.
Growth systems.
Brand energy.
Internet culture.
Web3.
AI.
Human psychology.

Individually:

## Designing conditions for connection.

The puzzle metaphor should appear subtly throughout the website:

* fragmented layouts
* quiet asymmetry
* hovering connections
* pieces slowly aligning
* systems revealing themselves over time

Never literal.
Never childish.

The metaphor should feel subconscious.
